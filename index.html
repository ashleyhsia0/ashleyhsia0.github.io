About

Hi, my name is Ashley and I am a software engineer.

With a background in marketing, business management, and web development, I am a marketer turned software engineer who designs and develops web applications from start to finish.

At a young age, I built websites as a hobby, back in the days when it was just static HTML/CSS and Notepad. In university, I took a few introductory computer science courses and excelled at them. Revisiting my interest in design and programming, I was self-taught before attending and completing an accelerated software engineering program at Hackbright Academy.

Now I am excited to leverage my business background and passion for technology into a software engineering role, whether it's full-stack, front-end, or back-end.

When I'm not coding, I'm eating and sleeping. I love eating and trying different kinds of cuisines - good food makes me happy! ♥
